      Scripts Involved & Responsibilities
GameManager
Controls main game states: MainMenu, Onboarding, Scenario1, Scenario2, Questionnaire, Credits, Paused
Handles state transitions
Saves and loads game progress
OnboardingController
Manages onboarding sequence
Spawns NPC Instructor
Plays dialogue and controls step progression
Saves progress at each step
ScenarioController
Manages scenario gameplay
Spawns Doctor, Interpreter, Crowd NPCs
Tracks player responses
Controls scene transitions
Saves progress at critical points
NPCInstructorController / NPCDoctorController / NPCInterpreterController / CrowdNPCController
Controls NPC-specific behavior:
Loads audio dynamically from Resources
Triggers animations
Plays dialogue audio
Sets text panel dialogue
TextPanelController (Singleton)
Manages UI for text displayed above NPC’s head:
Automatically follows NPC
Smoothly rotates to face player
Displays dialogue text dynamically
QuestionnaireController
Displays questions
Collects answers
Submits results
Saves progress
