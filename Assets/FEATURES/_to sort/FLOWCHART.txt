Scripts Involved & Responsibilities

GameManager
Controls main state (MainMenu, Onboarding, Scenario1, Scenario2, Questionnaire, Credits).

OnboardingController
Manages sequence of onboarding tutorial, initializes Instructor NPC, saves progress.

ScenarioController
Manages scenario gameplay (spawns NPCs, controls interactions, saves progress).

NPCInstructorController / NPCDoctorController / NPCInterpreterController / CrowdNPCController
Controls NPC-specific behavior:

Load audio dynamically from Resources
Trigger animations
Set dialogues
TextPanelController (Singleton)
Manages UI for text displayed above NPC’s head:

Automatically follows and smoothly rotates towards the player.
Shows appropriate dialogue text.
QuestionnaireController
Handles questionnaire interaction and saves answers.
